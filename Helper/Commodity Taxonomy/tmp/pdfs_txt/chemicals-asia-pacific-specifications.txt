         Specifications Guide            Asia-Pacific Chemicals: November 2025
         Definitions of the trading locations for which Platts publishes indexes or assessments
         The following guide contains the primary specifications for S&P Global Energy’s Platts Chemical assessments in the Americas. All the assessments listed here employ Platts Assessments Methodology, 
         as published in the Platts-Assessments-Methodology-Guide.pdf 
         These guides are designed to give Platts subscribers as much information as possible about a wide range of methodology and specifications. 
         This guide is current at the time of publication. Platts may issue further updates and enhancements to this methodology and will announce these to subscribers through its usual publications of record. 
         Such updates will be included in the next version of this guide. Platts editorial staff and managers are available to provide guidance when assessment issues require clarification. 
         Assessments are published in accordance with the Holiday Publication Schedule | S&P Global. Daily assessments are published on weekdays and are normally suspended in line with public holidays in 
         respective offices (Singapore, India). Weekly assessments are published on the specified days of the week, and are normally brought forward to the preceding working day, in case of a holiday.   
         The Market-on-Close assessment process for MTBE, benzene and paraxylene is facilitated through the eWindow communication tool. For further details, please consult the Chemicals timing and 
         increment guidelines at chemicals-timing.pdf
